# AnyCompany Airlines — Customer Service Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

AnyCompany Airlines is a full-service international carrier headquartered in Seoul, South Korea. We operate from our hub at Seoul Incheon International Airport (ICN) to over 95 international destinations across 7 regions. Our IATA code is AC and our ICAO code is ACY. Our SkyRewards loyalty programme has four tiers: Blue, Silver, Gold, and Platinum.

---

## 1. Booking & Reservations

### How do I book a flight?
You can book a flight through our website at anycompanyairlines.com, via the AnyCompany Airlines mobile app, by calling our reservations line at 1-800-555-0199 (available 24/7), or through an authorised travel agent. Online bookings receive a 5% SkyRewards bonus mile offer.

### Can I hold a booking without paying?
Yes. We offer a 24-hour hold on any booking made more than 7 days before departure. The hold is free of charge. After 24 hours the booking will be automatically cancelled if not confirmed with payment.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for bookings made more than 14 days in advance. Gift vouchers and SkyRewards miles can also be applied at checkout.

### Can I book a multi-city itinerary?
Yes. Select "Multi-city" on our booking page to add up to 5 flight segments in a single booking. Multi-city bookings are also available through our call centre.

### How do I book for a group?
Groups of 10 or more passengers can request a group booking quote through our Group Travel desk at groups@anycompanyairlines.com or by calling 1-800-555-0210. Group bookings must be made at least 21 days before departure.

### Can I request a specific seat?
Yes. Seat selection is available during booking or any time after via Manage My Booking. Preferred seats (extra legroom, window, aisle) may carry an additional fee depending on your fare class. SkyRewards Gold and Platinum members receive complimentary preferred seat selection.

## 2. Manage My Booking

### How do I change my flight?
Log into Manage My Booking on our website or app using your booking reference and last name. From there you can change dates, times, or routes subject to fare rules. Change fees depend on your fare type:
- Flex fare: No change fee
- Standard fare: $75 per change for domestic, $150 per change for international
- Basic fare: $150 per change for domestic, $250 per change for international
Any fare difference will also apply.

### How do I cancel my flight?
Cancellations can be made through Manage My Booking or by calling our reservations line. Refund eligibility depends on your fare type:
- Flex fare: Full refund to original payment method
- Standard fare: Refund minus cancellation fee ($100 domestic / $200 international)
- Basic fare: Non-refundable; a travel credit valid for 12 months will be issued minus the cancellation fee
All tickets are fully refundable within 24 hours of booking if the booking was made at least 7 days before departure.

### Can I change the passenger name?
Minor name corrections (up to 3 characters) can be made free of charge through Manage My Booking. Full name changes are not permitted; you would need to cancel and rebook.

### How do I add special meals?
Special meals can be requested through Manage My Booking at least 24 hours before departure. We offer: vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, gluten-free, diabetic, low-sodium, child meals, and baby meals.

### How do I request wheelchair assistance?
Wheelchair and mobility assistance can be requested during booking or through Manage My Booking at least 48 hours before departure. This service is free of charge. Please call our Special Assistance team at 1-800-555-0215 for complex mobility needs.

## 3. Check-In

### When can I check in online?
Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 1 hour before departure for domestic flights and 2 hours before departure for international flights. You can check in via our website, mobile app, or at airport self-service kiosks.

### What documents do I need at check-in?
- Domestic flights: A valid government-issued photo ID
- International flights: A valid passport (must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel date) and any required visas or travel authorisations for your destination
- For certain destinations, proof of vaccination or a negative COVID test may still be required — check our Travel Advisory page for the latest requirements

### Can I check in at the airport?
Yes. Airport check-in counters open 3 hours before departure for international flights and 2 hours before departure for domestic flights. Counters close 45 minutes before departure.

### I can't find my boarding pass. What do I do?
You can reprint or re-download your boarding pass anytime through Manage My Booking, the mobile app, or at an airport self-service kiosk. Your boarding pass is also available in Apple Wallet and Google Wallet.

## 4. Baggage

### What is the carry-on allowance?
All passengers may bring:
- 1 carry-on bag: max 55 x 40 x 20 cm (22 x 16 x 8 inches), max weight 10 kg (22 lbs)
- 1 personal item: handbag, laptop bag, or small backpack that fits under the seat in front

### What is the checked baggage allowance?
Checked baggage allowance depends on your fare type and route:

| Fare Type | Domestic | International (Economy) | International (Premium Economy) | International (Business) | International (First) |
|-----------|----------|------------------------|-------------------------------|------------------------|---------------------|
| Basic | 0 bags | 1 bag (23 kg) | 1 bag (23 kg) | 2 bags (32 kg each) | 3 bags (32 kg each) |
| Standard | 1 bag (23 kg) | 2 bags (23 kg each) | 2 bags (23 kg each) | 2 bags (32 kg each) | 3 bags (32 kg each) |
| Flex | 2 bags (23 kg each) | 2 bags (23 kg each) | 2 bags (23 kg each) | 3 bags (32 kg each) | 3 bags (32 kg each) |

Maximum dimensions per checked bag: 158 cm (62 inches) total (length + width + height).

### How much does extra baggage cost?
- Additional checked bag: $50 domestic / $75 international (per bag, per direction)
- Overweight bag (23–32 kg): $50 per bag
- Oversized bag (158–203 cm): $100 per bag
- SkyRewards Silver members and above receive 1 additional free checked bag

### What items are prohibited in carry-on baggage?
Prohibited carry-on items include: sharp objects (knives, scissors over 6 cm), sporting equipment (bats, clubs), tools (hammers, screwdrivers over 7 cm), flammable items, explosives, and liquids over 100 ml unless in a clear resealable bag (max 1 litre total). Lithium batteries must be in carry-on, not checked baggage.

### What if my baggage is delayed, damaged, or lost?
Report delayed or damaged baggage immediately at the Baggage Service counter in the arrivals hall before leaving the airport. You can also file a report online within 24 hours for delayed baggage and within 7 days for damaged baggage. For lost baggage (not located within 21 days), compensation will be provided in accordance with the Montreal Convention — up to approximately 1,288 SDR (roughly $1,700 USD). Keep all receipts for essential purchases made while waiting for delayed baggage, as we reimburse reasonable expenses up to $50/day for the first 5 days.

### Can I ship sports equipment or musical instruments?
Yes. Sports equipment such as golf clubs, skis, surfboards, and bicycles can be checked. Fees vary:
- Golf clubs / skis: Treated as one standard checked bag (applicable excess fees if over allowance)
- Surfboards / bicycles: $100 per item per direction
Musical instruments that fit in the cabin can be brought as carry-on. Larger instruments (e.g., cello) can be booked a seat — contact our reservations team.

## 6. In-Flight Services

### Is there Wi-Fi on board?
Yes. Wi-Fi is available on all long-haul aircraft and most short-haul aircraft. Plans start at $8.99 for messaging, $14.99 for browsing, and $21.99 for streaming for the full flight. Premium Economy, Business, and First Class passengers receive complimentary Wi-Fi. SkyRewards Gold and Platinum members also receive complimentary Wi-Fi in any cabin.

### What entertainment is available?
Our seatback entertainment system offers over 200 movies, 400 TV episodes, music, podcasts, games, and a real-time flight tracker. Content is refreshed monthly. Bluetooth headphone pairing is available on our A350 and 787 fleet.

### What meals are served?
- Short-haul (under 3 hours): Complimentary snack and beverages in all classes
- Medium-haul (3–6 hours): One complimentary meal service
- Long-haul (over 6 hours): Two complimentary meal services plus snacks available throughout the flight
All meals can be previewed on our website 2 weeks before departure. Special dietary meals must be ordered at least 24 hours in advance.

### Can I purchase duty-free on board?
Yes. Our in-flight duty-free catalogue features cosmetics, fragrances, electronics, watches, and Korean speciality items. Payment by card only. Pre-order online for guaranteed availability on your flight.

## 8. SkyRewards Loyalty Programme

### How do I join SkyRewards?
Membership is free. Sign up at anycompanyairlines.com/skyrewards, through our app, or at any airport check-in counter. You will receive a membership number immediately.

### How do I earn miles?
- Flying: Earn 1 mile per mile flown in Economy, 1.5x in Premium Economy, 2x in Business, and 3x in First Class
- Partner airlines: Earn miles on all SkyTeam alliance flights
- Credit card spending: Earn miles with our co-branded credit cards
- Partners: Hotels, car rentals, retail, and dining partners — full list at anycompanyairlines.com/skyrewards/partners

### What are the tier levels?
| Tier | Qualifying Miles / Flights per year | Key Benefits |
|------|-------------------------------------|-------------|
| Blue | 0 (entry) | Earn and redeem miles, member-only fares |
| Silver | 25,000 miles or 25 flights | Priority check-in, +1 checked bag, Economy lounge access |
| Gold | 50,000 miles or 50 flights | Business lounge access, complimentary Wi-Fi, priority boarding, +2 bags |
| Platinum | 100,000 miles or 100 flights | First Class lounge access, complimentary upgrades (subject to availability), chauffeur service, dedicated phone line |

### Do miles expire?
Miles expire after 36 months of account inactivity. Any qualifying activity (flight, purchase, or partner transaction) resets the 36-month clock for all miles in your account.

### How do I redeem miles?
Miles can be redeemed for:
- Award flights on AnyCompany Airlines and SkyTeam partners
- Cabin upgrades
- Excess baggage fees
- In-flight purchases and duty-free
- Hotel stays, car rentals, and experiences through our rewards catalogue
- Gift vouchers and charitable donations

### How do I transfer miles?
Miles can be transferred to another SkyRewards member for a fee of $15 per 1,000 miles, with a minimum transfer of 1,000 miles. Platinum members can transfer up to 50,000 miles per year free of charge.

## 9. Travel Documents & Visa Information

### What travel documents do I need?
All international passengers must carry a valid passport. Passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the travel date for most destinations. Visa requirements vary by nationality and destination. We recommend checking visa requirements at least 4 weeks before travel via our Visa Checker tool on the website or by contacting our customer service.

### Do you offer transit visa assistance?
For passengers transiting through Seoul Incheon, many nationalities can transit without a visa for up to 24 hours. Some nationalities may qualify for the Korea Transit Tourism Programme allowing up to 72 hours of visa-free stay. Check our Transit Information page for details.

### What about travelling with children?
- Children under 2 (infant): Travel on a parent's lap at 10% of the adult fare, or may occupy a seat at the child fare
- Children aged 2–11: Child fare (usually 75% of adult fare), full seat and baggage allowance
- Unaccompanied minors (5–11): Available on direct flights only, fee of $100 per direction, includes dedicated staff assistance from check-in to arrival
- Teens (12–17): Can travel alone with no additional fee but unaccompanied minor service is available on request

## 10. Delays, Cancellations & Disruptions

### What happens if my flight is delayed?
- Delay of 2+ hours: Complimentary refreshments provided
- Delay of 4+ hours: Meal voucher provided
- Delay of 6+ hours (or overnight): Hotel accommodation and ground transport arranged for eligible passengers
We will also offer rebooking on the next available AnyCompany Airlines or partner flight at no additional cost.

### What happens if my flight is cancelled?
If we cancel your flight, you are entitled to:
1. Rebooking on the next available flight at no cost
2. A full refund to your original payment method
3. If the cancellation occurs within 24 hours of departure: meal vouchers and hotel accommodation if applicable
Compensation may also be payable depending on applicable consumer protection regulations in your country of departure.

### What about missed connections?
If you miss a connecting flight due to a delay on a previous AnyCompany Airlines flight, we will automatically rebook you on the next available connection at no cost. Proceed to the nearest transfer desk or customer service counter. If an overnight stay is required, accommodation will be arranged.

## 11. Special Assistance & Accessibility

### Do you accommodate passengers with disabilities?
Yes. We provide wheelchair assistance, accessible seating, onboard wheelchair-accessible lavatories on wide-body aircraft, and trained cabin crew to assist passengers with reduced mobility. Service animals are welcome on board with advance notice (at least 48 hours).

### Can I travel with medical equipment?
Yes. CPAP machines, portable oxygen concentrators (POCs), and other approved medical devices can be used on board. Notify us at least 48 hours before departure. POCs must be FAA-approved. We provide in-seat power for medical devices.

### Do you accommodate allergies?
We cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment, but we take reasonable precautions. If you have a severe nut allergy, notify us at booking and again at check-in. We will make an announcement to fellow passengers and refrain from serving nut products in your cabin section.

## 12. Refunds & Compensation

### How do I request a refund?
Refund requests can be submitted through Manage My Booking or by contacting our customer service. Processing times:
- Credit card refunds: 7–14 business days
- Travel credits: Issued within 24 hours
- Bank transfers: 14–21 business days

### What is your compensation policy for overbooking?
If you are involuntarily denied boarding due to overbooking, you are entitled to:
- Rebooking on the next available flight
- Compensation of 200% of your one-way fare (up to $675) if rebooked within 2 hours of original arrival, or 400% (up to $1,350) if delayed beyond 2 hours
- Meal and lounge access while waiting

## 14. Frequently Asked Questions — Quick Reference

**Q: Can I bring my pet on board?**
A: Small cats and dogs (under 8 kg including carrier) are allowed in the cabin on select routes for a fee of $125 per direction. Larger pets travel in our temperature-controlled cargo hold for $200–$400 depending on size and route. Service animals fly free. Book pet travel at least 72 hours in advance.

**Q: Is there a fee for printing a boarding pass at the airport?**
A: No. Boarding pass printing at the airport is always free at our counters and self-service kiosks.

**Q: Can I upgrade my cabin after booking?**
A: Yes. Upgrade options may be available through Manage My Booking, at the airport, or at the gate. You can upgrade using cash, SkyRewards miles, or a combination. Bid upgrades are also available on select routes starting 72 hours before departure.

**Q: What is your policy on flight credits?**
A: Flight credits are valid for 12 months from the date of issue, applicable to any AnyCompany Airlines flight. Credits are non-transferable and can be used for the full booking amount including taxes and fees.

**Q: Do you offer travel insurance?**
A: Yes. Comprehensive travel insurance is available during booking through our partner, SkyProtect Insurance. Coverage includes trip cancellation, medical emergencies, baggage loss, and travel delays. Prices start from $29 per trip.

**Q: What is the dress code for lounges?**
A: Smart casual attire is required. Swimwear, sleeveless shirts, and flip-flops are not permitted in our Business and First Class lounges.
